Welcome to MAAN Hospital, a multispecialty care hospital running under the aegis of Maan Welfare Society.
Maan Welfare Society the promoting society is registered under Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860 vides Registration No. 1125 of 1992-93. It was incorporated with the laudable objective of advancement of Health care Education and Research & Development in North India. The society is running several institutions with mission and vision to provide services and opportunities with excellence in the area of education and research.
When we started our first multi-specialty referral hospital in 1989, our mission was to provide quality healthcare of international standards in India. Our consistent commitment in maintaining excellence has made us the largest healthcare provider in Haryana/Punjab. Today, Maan Hospital is a world in itself. We manage a network of specialty and super specialty hospitals and clinics, and provide technical, medical & paramedical education. After more than twenty-five years of dedicated service, I feel, we still have a lot to achieve.
