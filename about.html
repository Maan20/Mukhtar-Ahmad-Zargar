Maan Hospital

The seeds of our origin were sown as early as 2000 when the founder of the Maan Group (MG), Dr. ArMaan , established the Maan Hospital Patiala, Punjab. Maan Hospitals as an entity came into existence in 2004 with 650-bed flagship hospital at Patiala. Today, we are one of India’s leading healthcare groups with over 7000 beds and have an international presence.
Our core values are built around the thought of patient-first and that each doctor at Maan Hospitals is a human care expert, going above and beyond the call of duty as they live by the belief that every single life is priceless. When they embark on these journeys, stories emerge - stories of grit, determination and never giving up. Join us on a journey to discover stories that reinforce your belief in 'Life's On'
